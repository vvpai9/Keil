The 2-nd level Bootloader for Freescale 'MCIMX27'  
device series (configured for External SDRAM 
and loaded to External NAND Flash on the 
Phytec Board)

This is a 2-nd level bootloader for Freescale 
'MCIMX27' device series with External NAND 
Flash and External SDRAM. 
It downloads to first 2kB of the NAND Flash.
The 1-st level bootloader that is in ROM of 
MCIMX27 first downloads this 2-nd level 
bootloader to internal NAND RAM and starts it.

The 2-nd level bootloader's main function is to 
copy user program from the External NAND Flash 
to the External SDRAM and start it.

Just select Flash -> Download to download it 
to the NAND Flash.

Notes:
  Jumper 'Boot Mode/CLK-Sel.' (S5) has to be in 
  position: 4,6,8 = ON, other = OFF
